Hyper Taiwan Technology has developed various test equipment suites to support the various stages of test during the Bluetooth module/product mass production process. They include firmware burning and downloading , module/product function test, RF test etc.
  • Chips Supported：BroadCom, CSR, Microtune, SiliconWave, TI etc.
  • Product/function Supported ：Mono/Stereo Headset, Handsfree, Dongle(MP3 Player), Car Kit etc.
  • RF Instrument supported：CBT32(R&S), MT8852B(Anritsu), N4010(Agilent) etc.
The customers of Hyper Taiwan Technology cover all the famous international companies such as Sennheiser communications,Denmark, Flextronics, Delta etc.

  • Module/PCBA Functional and RF Testing Station
    1. RF Performance Trim and Calibration
    2. Module Functional Test
    3. RF TX/RX Testing
